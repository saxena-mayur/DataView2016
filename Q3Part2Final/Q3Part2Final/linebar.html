Which regions do better than others in controlling diseases relative to money?




Question: Which regions do better than others in controlling diseases relative to money?

Data Sets

  • H-DS-1: All India (from 2000 to 2011) and State-wise (2010 and 2011) number of cases and deaths due to specified diseases (Acute Diarrhoeal Diseases, Malaria, Acute Respiratory Infection, Japanese Encephalitis, Viral Hepatitis),
  • F-DS-5: State-wise plan Outlays and Expenditure on Health (2002-2007) Tenth Plan
  • Data:Primary Census Abstract 2011 â€“ India,

Methodology

  • Two possible scenarios to infer that a disease is controlled area:
    • Reduced numbers of cases
    • Increased cured cases count
  • For every disease, first we compute:
    • number of cured cases = number of cases - number of deaths
  • For every state:
    • normalize Total Expenditure and the cured cases and overall cases count by dividing it by total population of that state
    • Consider the ratio Rcases = normalized cases toll/normalized Total Expenditure = (#Cases/Person) / (Total Expenditure/Person) to infer trends visually.
    Consider the ratio RCured = (normalized cured cases toll/normalized Total Expenditure) = (#Cured Cases/Person) / (Total Expenditure/Person) to infer trends visually.
