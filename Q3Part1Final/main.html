How much money has been allocated to tackle specific diseases compared to others?


Question: How much money has been allocated to tackle specific diseases compared to others?

Data Sets

  • F-DS-3: Outlays for Department of Health as agreed by planning commission during Tenth Plan.
  • F-DS-6: Outlay Tenth Plan, Tenth Plan (2002-07) sum of Annual Outlay and Tenth Plan (2002-07) Actual Expenditure for Department of Health and Family Welfare
  • Data: Outlay and Actual Expenditure other than NRHM during the Tenth Plan (2002-2007) for Department of Health and Family Welfare

Methodology

  • Extract the expenditures incurred during 9th and 10th plan for the various diseases from the datasets.
  • Expenditures considered are:
    • 9th Plan Allocation, 10th Plan Allocation Agreed by PC and Sum of Actual Expenditure for 10th Plan
  • Diseases Considered are:
    • Vector-borne, Leprosy, AIDS, Guinea Worm, Disease Surveillance, Blindness, Cancer, IDD, Mental health, De-addiction, Tuberculosis, Leprosy Institutes(T), BCG, Pulse Polio
